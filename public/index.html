BRUNO MONTEZANO

Psychologist and Data Analyst

I’m currently pursuing my Master’s degree in Psychiatry and Behavioral Sciences at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul. I earned my Bachelor of Science in Psychology from the Catholic University of Pelotas (2021). I also earned my Computer Technician degree from the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Rio Grande do Sul (2015). I perform data analysis with big data and bleeding edge machine learning techniques within the mental health area, what’s being called as “precision psychiatry”. I’m a big fan of FOSS technologies, Linux, statistical programming, reproducible science, chess and a nice tea.
